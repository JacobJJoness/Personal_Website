Annual Surface Temperature Averages Since 1880

Data Report Breakdown

This project takes raw data, in the form of a csv file, and utilizes the chart.js library to display data in a more useful format. While the project is fairly small I do see very valuable point to be made. The small set of data I pulled from still manages to reflect certain facts, and prove really powerful. See below.

  • • Between 1940-1946 (World War II) there is a sudden dramatic increase in temperature.
  • • The dramatic increase in temperature since 1970, also reflects the growth of the Global Economy.
  • • Before the industrial revolution we can see a regular pattern of warm and cold fluctuations.
