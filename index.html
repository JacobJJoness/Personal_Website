About Me

Howdy, my name is Jacob Jones, and yes I do say hello like that. As you may guess I am from South Texas, in a coastal city called Corpus Christi. Most things to do growing up in Corpus were fishing, at home hobbies, and a lot of various restaurants I mean like a lot. That being said I had to grow up creative, and a lot of my creativity is born of my natural curiosities and enjoyment of learning. I got into programming my second year of highschool, and have maintained steady coursework to improve my skill since then. However, despite this I would honestly say my biggest passion is problem solving, and then coding of course. I truly see my future profession as an artform beautifully contrived using practical mathematics, and logic. Outside of my degree, I enjoy drawing, cooking, fishing, gardening, reading, etc. I'd like to consider myself a jack of all trades, and a lifelong learner. My dream is to become a software developer for Amazon. Mainly, due to the fact I feel they will be a workplace where I am always challenged to learn new things, and keep a forward thinking mindset and appreciate the final product for the end user. Please enjoy the rest of the site. There are many small details etched to this work in progress site.
